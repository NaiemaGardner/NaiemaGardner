Hello there! Naiema Gardner here, software developer based in the New York metropolitan area. I come equipped with a set of fresh eyes, cultivated skills and immensive enthusiasm to learn and grow in the ever expanding worlds of technology and software engineering.

My educational background in Psychology and working experience in the fields of Human Services and Customer Care have allowed me to specialize in methodical planning and project implementation, as well as highly effective communication and collaboration techniques. I most look forward to bringing my compassionate temperament, collective growth mindset and mindful creativity to the scope of application development.

When taking a break from coding, I love to indulge in the tranquility and beauty of the natural world while hiking mountains or swimming in seas. I am a protector of human and animal rights, a photography and design enthusiast, and I always enjoy a good puzzle. A true logophile who writes daily and aspires to publish self-help rhetoric as well as works of poetry and short stories someday. My vision is a life of service where I can contribute to the upliftment of others through innovative technology and socioemotional support.
